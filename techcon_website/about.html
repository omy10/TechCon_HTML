Our History

TechCon began in 2012 as a small gathering of passionate developers and has grown into one of the most respected global technology conferences.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to inspire innovation, foster community, and drive the future of technology forward through collaboration and education.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed many influential voices in the tech industry.

  • Elena Navarro – AI Researcher, known for groundbreaking work in ethical machine learning.
  • James Liu – Lead Engineer at SkyTech, innovator in cloud security.
  • Sophia Mendez – CEO of CodeBridge, advocate for women in STEM.
